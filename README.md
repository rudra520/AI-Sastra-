## 🌟 Vision
**AI-Sastra** is a comprehensive web directory designed to bridge the gap between complex technology and everyday human needs. We believe that Artificial Intelligence should not be a privilege for the tech-savvy, but a utility for all. 

Our mission is to provide a curated, intuitive ecosystem tailored for specific user personas:
* **🎓 Students:** Academic research aids, coding assistants, and personalized learning paths.
* **💼 Professionals:** Workflow automation, productivity enhancers, and industry-specific LLMs.
* **👴 Seniors:** Simplified, high-accessibility interfaces designed to reduce the digital divide.

## ✨ Core Features (In Development)
- **🔍 Intelligent Directory:** A multi-layered filtering system to find the exact AI tool for your specific task.
- **📚 AI Literacy Modules:** Step-by-step guides on prompt engineering and maximizing AI utility.
- **🤝 Community Hub:** User-driven reviews and recommendations to verify tool efficacy.
- **♿ Accessibility Suite:** Specialized UI/UX features (high contrast, voice-guided navigation) for senior citizens.
